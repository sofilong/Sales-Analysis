<h1>Sales and Revenue Report</h1>
<p>
    
This dashboard unlocks insights on sales data to help identify trends, behavioral patterns, in order to understand what drive sales, reinforce and strengthen sales strategies to improve revenue performance, know your top Customer to help keep and build a good and strong bisiness relationship.
    </p>

<h2>Why is the analysis for?</h2>
<p>
  Sales is what makes any business profitable, is the oil that keeps the engine running in any Organization, so knowing sales behavior is key! This dashboard helps answer that by breaking down into specific level of details to visualize where sales thrives, what are the top Customers to focus on building and strengthening a good relationship, a map showing market presence by state with gradient colors to help visualize what region can be used to pilot new sales strategies vs region where sales continue to thrive.  <img src="Sales Report.jpg" alt="Dashboard viz">
</p>
